<p>This Exercise is as simple as it is effective:

<h2>Action:</h2>
<p>- smile -
<p>If you have a mirror, smile at yourself. And try to smile with your eyes as well.
<p>Hold it for a couple of seconds.
<p>Repeat as often as you like.
<p>The chemicals in your brain will react as if you would be smiling naturally.
<p>This gives your mood a little boost.
